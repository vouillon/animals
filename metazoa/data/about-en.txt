<h1>A Tree of Animals</h1>

This <a target="_blank"
href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Phylogenetic_tree">
<em>phylogenetic</em> tree</a> displays the relationships among
animals.

<h2>Usage</h2>

Browse the tree by dragging it with the mouse. Click on any image to
display a larger version.
